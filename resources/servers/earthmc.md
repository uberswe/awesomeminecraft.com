# EarthMC

**Website:** [https://earthmc.net](https://earthmc.net)

**Category:** Servers > Geopolitical Servers

## Overview

A compelling geopolitical server featuring a 1:500 scale Earth map generated from satellite data. Players establish towns, form nations, and engage in diplomacy. Over 3 million players since 2016.

## Features

- 1:500 scale Earth map from satellite data
- Establish towns and form nations
- Diplomacy and nation-building gameplay
- 250-400 concurrent players
- Over 3 million total players since 2016
